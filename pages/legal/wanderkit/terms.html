2. Description of Service

WanderKit is a travel planning application that allows you to:

  • Create and manage trips with packing lists, itineraries, and journals
  • Track flights and receive departure/arrival reminders
  • Manage travel budgets and log expenses
  • Store passport and document details on-device
  • Explore destinations, live weather, currency rates, and travel tips
  • Export and back up your data locally

Data Export Options:

Standard export (.json): Readable on any device — use this when switching phones.

Encrypted export (.wkb): Uses AES-256 encryption — can only be decrypted on the originating device. More secure, but device-specific.

Always export a backup before deleting the app or switching devices. We cannot recover lost data.
