Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

This privacy policy applies to the McJohnson website at mcjohnson.website ("the Site"). McJohnson is a one-person indie software studio based in Poland, European Union.

For privacy questions, contact: support@mcjohnson.website

2. What This Policy Covers

This policy covers the McJohnson website only — what data is collected when you visit, how it is used, and your rights. It does not cover the mobile apps (Pefi, Yearbook). Each app has its own privacy policy accessible from within the app and from its page on this site.

3. Data We Collect

When you visit this site, the following may be collected automatically:

  • Pages visited and time spent (via Google Analytics 4)
  • Approximate location (country/city level, not precise)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referral source (how you arrived at the site)
  • Outbound link clicks (including affiliate link clicks)

When you use the contact form:

  • Your name and email address
  • The content of your message

We do not collect payment information. Advertising payments are handled externally via Stripe or bank transfer.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To understand how the site is used and improve it
  • To respond to contact form submissions
  • To report aggregate traffic data to advertisers (no personal data is shared)
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Affiliate Links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, the linked provider may set cookies on your browser to attribute the sale. Each provider's privacy policy governs their cookie use. We earn a small commission from qualifying purchases at no extra cost to you.

We do not receive any personal data about you from affiliate purchases — only whether a qualifying purchase occurred.

6. Google Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised usage statistics. Google Analytics uses cookies. The data collected is aggregated and does not identify individual users. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We share read-only access to Google Analytics data with paying advertisers so they can verify their ad performance. This data is aggregated and contains no personal information.

7. Cookies

This site uses cookies only for Google Analytics. No advertising cookies, no tracking pixels, no third-party marketing cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google (for Analytics — see section 6)
  • When required by law

We do not share your data with any third-party AI systems.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

As we are based in the EU, all users are protected under GDPR. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, email support@mcjohnson.website with the subject "GDPR Request".

10. Data Retention

Contact form emails are retained only as long as needed to respond. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months per our GA4 settings.

11. Children

This site is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact

Email: support@mcjohnson.website
