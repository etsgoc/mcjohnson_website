YEARBOOK

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Yearbook. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data We Collect

Yearbook is designed with privacy in mind.

Locally stored data (on your device — never transmitted to us):

  • Your name and profile information
  • Journal entries
  • Events and memories
  • Routines and habits
  • Secrets (encrypted)
  • Photos and videos you add
  • Links you save
  • App operational data (subscription dates, feature usage timestamps)

Data sent to our servers:

  • Email address (only when you contact support)
  • Subscription verification data (payment receipts from Apple/Google, device ID)
  • Support inquiry content (when you contact us)

3. Data Backup & Export

Yearbook offers two types of backups:

Option 1 — Local Device Backup (Automatic)

When you enable "Local Device Backup" in Settings, we create an encrypted ZIP backup file on your device. This file is included in your device's automatic system backup only if you have enabled device backups in your phone settings:

  • iOS: Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > iCloud Backup (must be ON)
  • Android: Settings > Google > Backup (must be ON)

We do NOT directly sync to iCloud or Google Drive. To restore, you must restore your entire device from backup.

Option 2 — Manual Export (Portable)

Go to Settings > Export Data:

  • "Encrypted (Same Device Only)": More secure, works only on this device
  • "Unencrypted (Any Device)": Can be imported on any device

Manual exports create ZIP files you save wherever you choose. We have no access to these files.

⚠ If you have NOT enabled device backups in your phone settings, your data will NOT be backed up to the cloud, even if you enable "Local Device Backup" in our app.

Recommendation: Use "Unencrypted (Any Device)" export if you plan to switch phones or keep long-term backups.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Local Storage: All personal data is stored on your device with device-level encryption
  • Server Data: Email (support only), subscription verification data, device ID
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data
  • No Advertising: We never share your data with advertisers
  • No AI: We do not share your personal data with any third-party AI systems

5. Data Security

  • All secrets are encrypted using device-level encryption
  • Local backup files are encrypted end-to-end
  • Biometric authentication protects sensitive sections
  • Your data never leaves your device without your explicit consent

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time
  • Export your data in ZIP format
  • Delete all your data
  • Disable local backups at any time
  • Request account and server-side data deletion

To request deletion of your data: Email support@mcjohnson.website with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will delete all server-side data (subscription records, device ID) within 30 days. Local device data is deleted by uninstalling the app.

7. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

As we are based in the European Union, all users are protected under GDPR. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

The only personal data we process is your email address (if you contact support) and subscription verification data. All other data remains on your device.

8. Subscription Data

  • Payment processing is handled by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android)
  • We do not store your payment information
  • We store encrypted subscription verification data (receipt tokens, device ID) solely to validate your subscription

9. Children's Privacy

Yearbook is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date in the app.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or to submit a GDPR data request, please contact us at:

support@mcjohnson.website
