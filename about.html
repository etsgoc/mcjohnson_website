About McJohnson

An indie developer building focused, privacy-first mobile apps.

What This Is

McJohnson is a one-person indie studio building mobile apps for everyday life. The apps are designed to be simple, private, and genuinely useful — no unnecessary tracking, no data farming, no ads inside the apps.

This website exists to showcase those apps, share tech products I genuinely recommend through affiliate links, and offer ad space for brands that want to reach a focused tech audience.

Based in Poland, EU. GDPR compliant. Building in public.
